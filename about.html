Company Overview

Cowry Treasurers Limited, a subsidiary of Cowry Asset Management Limited was incorporated as a limited liability company in Nigeria on March 2, 2012 and subsequently licensed as Funds Managers by Securities and Exchange Commission on March 28, 2018. The Company which has a paid-up capital of N150 million is a member of Cowry Group with a combined group paid up capital in excess of N1.5 billion and asset under management of over N10billion. Cowry Treasurers as a wealth management company is home to all categories of investors and forms of investments services across fixed income securities, variable income assets (equities, exchange traded funds, commodities etc), specialized investment and alternative assets such as REITS, private equity, infrastructure funds among others. The company has as its twin thrust objective capital preservation and optimal return on investment. Cowry Treasurers is avowed to delivering exceptional returns without unduly exposing the investments to high risks leveraging on our robust risk management framework, the expertise and track record of the team members. Our Investment Managers have consistently outperformed their peers having carved a niche for themselves in the industry with a long-standing cumulative experience of 120 years in delivering superior returns. The Directors of the Company are highly endowed with diverse professional competencies, experiences and cutting-edge expertise both in the financial and non-financial services sectors of the economy complemented by their experiences from other board appointments. The company has in place a very sound corporate governance policy and practice with strong passion for ethical practice. We do not only talk corporate governance; we walk the talk. Our Investment Committee has as its chairman an independent member in addition to another independent member as an alternate chairman to the committee. Also, the Risk Management Committee is headed by an independent member. As a company, Cowry Treasurers is constantly seeking for opportunity to delight our customers by providing excellent customer service delivery supported by our state-of-the-art technology in information technology blended with our customized products. Our vision is to be the foremost pan-African wealth creating financial services provider of global repute while our mission is to create exceptional wealth for all stakeholders. At Cowry our core values are Professionalism, Service excellence, High ethical standard, Respect for the individual, Team work and Social responsibility. We are more than just Funds Managers; we are a wealth creating partner. Open an account with us today.
